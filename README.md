# Palethea Launcher

Palethea Launcher is a modern Minecraft launcher built for players who want full control without friction.

From clean instance organization to deep modpack flows, Palethea keeps everything in one fast desktop app.

Built with Tauri + React. Currently targeting Windows and Linux.

![Palethea Launcher](https://img.shields.io/badge/Tauri-2.0-blue) ![React](https://img.shields.io/badge/React-19-61dafb)

## What You Can Do

- **Organize your whole library** with custom instance names, colors, and per-instance settings.
- **Set up mod loaders fast** with built-in Fabric, Forge, and NeoForge installation.
- **Install modpacks from both ecosystems** using **Modrinth** and **CurseForge** sources.
- **Manage content in one place**: mods, resource packs, shaders, and datapacks.
- **Import existing setups** from Palethea exports and PrismLauncher `.zip` files.
- **Rebuild shared setups** using share codes for quick multiplayer parity.
- **Choose launch identity per instance** with account pinning, or use your global active account.
- **Play Microsoft or offline** depending on your workflow.
- **Track your time** with library-wide playtime and usage stats.
- **Launch faster** with desktop shortcuts for specific instances.
- **Keep working while tasks run** thanks to integrated progress tracking for setup/install flows.

## Why Palethea

- **Fast native feel** from Tauri architecture.
- **Built for modded players**, not just vanilla launch.
- **Clear, focused UI** for high-frequency daily use.
- **Practical power features** without overcomplicated setup.
